Rules

Life is directly related to the colored portion of circle.

Once you loose all your life, you're out

Last man standing wins.

Procedure:

  1. Roll the die by clicking on it
  2. Move as many spaces shown on die
    • If you land on a black space: That space becomes under your control
    • If you land on another players space: You loose one life; answer a question if you answer correctly, it becomes you space.
    • If you land on a star you gain 3 life
